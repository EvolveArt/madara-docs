# Data Availability

Leveraging its foundation on Starknet, Madara has inherent support for a multifaceted Data Availability (DA) ecosystem.
This empowers developers to choose from a diverse range of DA layers, including established options like Avail, Celestia, and the Ethereum L1, with further integrations actively underway.

Within this section, you'll find tutorials guiding you through the seamless integration of your app chain with these DA layers.
